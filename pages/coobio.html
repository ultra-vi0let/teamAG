Adam Ferkowicz

Chief Operating Officer



Adam is a pragmatic and enterprising professional with success streamlining operations, implementing process improvements, and planning complex systems. Adam has proven himself at building and cultivating lasting partnerships and loyal teams, ensuring financial and professional growth.
